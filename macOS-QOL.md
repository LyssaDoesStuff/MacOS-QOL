# MacOS QOL Applications
*Written by Lyssa Tompkins, Updated April 2026*

**A nice collection of macOS apps I use daily on my MacBook, that I find make the experience much better!**

*Credit to Snazzy Labs for a majority of these applications, he makes fantastic app suggestion videos.*


# Background Improvements

**Apps that are not directly opened, but provide small background improvements to your experience**

## Shottr

Free screenshot app, that offers much more functionality than the default. Can be remapped to cmd+shift+4 (Technically paid, but free version is fantastic!)

https://shottr.cc

## Quickshade

Macro based artificial dimmer. Does not dim backlight, but can make the display dimmer than default options, especially for third party displays

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/quickshade/id931571202

## Pure Paste

Strips text formatting from clipboard by default

https://sindresorhus.com/pure-paste

## Pippo

Adds seek buttons to macOS PiP

https://goodsnooze.gumroad.com/l/pippo

## MonitorControl

Adds native brightness controls to most monitors (Display must support DDC/CI)

https://github.com/MonitorControl/MonitorControl/releases

## MiddleClick

Replicates scroll wheel clicks using a three-finger click on trackpad

https://github.com/artginzburg/MiddleClick

## LinearMouse

Per device settings (Disable natural scrolling just for mouse, etc.)

https://linearmouse.app/en/

## HyperKey

Transforms caps lock key into a modifier key for macros

https://hyperkey.app

## Hidden Bar

Allows for you to hide menu bar icons

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/hidden-bar/id1452453066

## FluxMarkdown

Adds md file support to macOS

https://github.com/xykong/flux-markdown

## DockDoor

Better Alt+Tab for macOS

https://dockdoor.net

## CopyClip

Adds clipboard history to menu bar, feels native

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/copyclip-clipboard-history/id595191960

## CheatSheet

Shows available hotkeys for an app when command key is pressed

https://cheatsheet-mac.en.softonic.com/mac

## Alcove (Paid)

Adds Dynamic Island (Notch) to macOS

https://tryalcove.com

# QOL Apps

**These apps are more in the foreground, and have a UI associated with them beyond just settings. Useful for a variety of things!**

## TypeWhisper

Much better transcription/dictation for macOS. Uses on device AI, but can be connected to online LLMs.

https://www.typewhisper.com/en/

## TinkerTool

Adds extra system settings to your device

https://www.bresink.com/osx/TinkerTool.html

## Startup Disk Full Fixer

Removes cache files to free up system storage

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/startup-disk-full-fixer/id1346655838

## Shortery (Paid)

Improves Siri Shortcuts, allows for triggers based on time of day, application open/close, hotkey, etc.

https://apps.apple.com/us/app/shortery/id1594183810

## OBS

Better screen recording, including system and microphone audio.

https://obsproject.com

## Latest

App Updater for various non App Store apps

https://github.com/mangerlahn/Latest

## ImageOptim

Fine tuned image compression, good for size capped image uploads on platforms like Discord

https://imageoptim.com/mac

## CoconutBattery

Device battery health & information, for your Mac & any USB connected devices

https://coconut-flavour.com/coconutbattery/

## AntiNote (Paid)

Hotkey enabled advanced quick notes, good for jotting down thoughts and reminders for later

https://antinote.io
